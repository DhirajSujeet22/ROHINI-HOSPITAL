    *Registry of Excluded Hospitals*

    Welcome to the official registry of hospitals excluded from coverage under policies provided by Nationalized Indian Insurance Company. This registry aims to provide transparency and clarity to policyholders regarding healthcare institutions that are currently not covered under our health insurance plans.

    • *Important Notes:*
    • The exclusion may be due to various reasons such as non-compliance with our quality standards, breach of agreement, or administrative policies.
    • - This list is regularly updated to ensure that our customers have access to the latest information regarding coverage.
    • - We encourage our policyholders to verify the coverage of hospitals before seeking medical services.

    At Nationalized Indian Insurance Company, we strive to ensure that you receive the best care possible under our coverage. The exclusion of certain hospitals is based on strict guidelines that prioritize patient safety, healthcare quality, and compliance with industry regulations.

    *Benefits of the Registry of Excluded Hospitals*

    The *Registry of Excluded Hospitals* ensures transparency and helps policyholders make informed decisions regarding their healthcare. By accessing this regularly updated list, customers can avoid unexpected expenses from seeking treatment at non-covered hospitals. This registry also reflects our commitment to maintaining high standards of care by excluding hospitals that do not meet our quality or regulatory benchmarks. Ultimately, it protects policyholders from substandard services while guiding them toward covered, trusted healthcare providers The *Registry of Hospitals Excluded by Nationalized Indian Insurance Company (ROHENII)* is a critical resource for policyholders seeking to make informed decisions about their healthcare coverage.     *Why ROHENII Matters:*

    1. *Transparency*: ROHENII provides clear information, ensuring that policyholders know which hospitals are not covered, thus preventing unexpected medical bills
    2. *Informed Healthcare Decisions*: By consulting ROHENII, customers can select hospitals that are part of their insurance network, ensuring smoother claims processing.
    3. *Updated Information*: The registry is continuously updated to reflect the most recent changes in hospital coverage, helping customers stay informed.
    4. *Focus on Quality*: Excluded hospitals often fail to meet the stringent quality or service standards set by the insurance provider, safeguarding policyholders from substandard care.

    *How to Use ROHENII*:

    Simply search by hospital name or location to determine if your preferred healthcare facility is covered under your policy. We encourage all policyholders to review this registry before seeking treatment, ensuring they receive the full benefits of their health insurance.
